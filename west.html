      {% translate "Also translated as The Decline of the Chinese Empire. Accustomed to reading the chronicles of modern Chinese history, looking at this modern history book will naturally give you a refreshing feeling. So, if it's not based on time, where is the focus of the entire book? The chronological style is based on time, while the biographical style is based on characters. And this book is based on three social groups, namely farmers, gentry, and merchants. The fundamental purpose of writing in this way is still to showcase the undercurrents within society. Talking about farmers, revealing land and production relations, as well as the impact on society, emphasizing the role of literati groups and the elite class in society. Speaking of merchants, one naturally wants to find the driving force of the economy on society and the emergence of capitalism. And these three are closely linked together, and on top of them, Chinese modern history is derived. Readers will naturally have different feelings about the modern history written in this way." %}
